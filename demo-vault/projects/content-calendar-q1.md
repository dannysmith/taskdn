## Overview

Create a content calendar for Q1 2025 to maintain consistent visibility and build audience.

## Channels

1. **Blog** - Long-form content (2x per month)
2. **Twitter/X** - Daily engagement, weekly threads
3. **LinkedIn** - Professional updates (weekly)
4. **Newsletter** - Monthly roundup

## Q1 Themes

- January: New year, goals, fresh starts
- February: Workflow and productivity
- March: Learning and skill development

## Content Ideas

### January
- [ ] 2025 goals and focus areas
- [ ] Tool/stack updates for the new year

### February
- [ ] How I structure my work week
- [ ] Favorite productivity tools

### March
- [ ] Learning in public retrospective
- [ ] Q1 wrap-up and Q2 preview

## Tracking

Use simple spreadsheet to track:
- Planned vs published
- Engagement metrics
- What's working
